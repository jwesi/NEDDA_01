Dear You,

Thank you for allowing me to live in this fantasy for a while. Thank you for putting a face on my formless vision of love. Even if it was only for a while. Even if you didn’t really do much to fulfill the role I wanted. You were never obligated to. I don’t blame you for not doing so, not at all. I hope to leave this situation a little bit better. I hope to stop playing the games we did. They don’t serve either of us. We both lost in the end. I know I’m not over you, not entirely. I am over this odd feeling of ambiguity in terms of our relationship. Not really friends not at all lovers not really anything. Awkward interactions at the places we frequent. Painful glances at you being happy without me. I would like to put that behind us. I still want to forgive you and teach you how to better understand me. I want to learn how to better understand you. Yet, I cannot allow myself to walk this path alone. I will not pretend that I am some sort of martyr. A blamless victim that has been snubbed by love. I am not either of those things. I was an active player in this game. I know I’ve done things to confuse you. Sometimes on purpose. Now I no longer wish to play these games. I know it will still hurt at those places we frequent. I know I will still act in ways that don’t match my words. I will still think about the times we spent together. I will still get high and think about you. Still get drunk and wish you were holding me. Wish you made me feel less lonley. Now is not one of those times. Now I write to you fully sober to tell you, while I still have feelings for you I am no longer afraid of letting go. I no longer wish to hold on to a fantasy of you stepping up and being what I need. Or me suddenly fulfilling your dreams of love either. I will not fight for this anymore. I won’t struggle for a place in your heart. I won’t struggle to be yours. Nor will I fight with the fact that I am not yours. If you want me, in any form, I would apprieciate some form of action. As I have tried to take action. If not, I will allow things to fall as they should. I will allow this to be what it was.

I will no longer be inspired by this heartache.

Jwesi

p.s. I’m probably lying. There really is no way to not be inspired by heartache.
