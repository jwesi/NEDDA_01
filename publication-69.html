There’s something about the lighting

It’s off to the right by just too much.

Or maybe it’s a bit too bright.

Something about the lighting

makes me feel

like reality

has shifted.

THE CYCLE NEVER ENDS. LIVING SIMPLY BECAUSE ONE DAY YOU WOKE UP AND LOOKED INTO THE MIRROR AND SAID “THAT IS I AND I AM ME. THIS PIECE OF GLASS REFLECTS MYSELF BACK INTO MY OWN IRISES.” YOU WILL REALIZE THAT YOUR ACTIONS HAVE CONSINQUENCES. YOU TOUCH THE CATS TAIL, YOU GET SCRATCHED. THROW THE BOTTLE OF NAIL POLISH AT YOUR MOTHER, SHE THROWS IT BACK. THEN BREAKS A PHONE ON YOUR HEAD. FUCK SOMEONE OF THE OPISITE SEX WHO IS VIRILE AND ABLE TO REPRODUCE AND THEY MAY GET PREGNANT. GIVE BIRTH TO LIFE WHO WILL THEN LOOK INTO THE MIRROR AND SAY “ THAT IS I AND I AM ME. THIS PIECE OF GLASS REFLECTS MYSELF BACK INTO MY OWN IRISES.” THEY WILL THEN REALIZE THAT THEIR ACTIONS HAVE CONSINQUENCES.
