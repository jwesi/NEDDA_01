Should I distract myself so I can stay? Or float away to places out of my control Places I may not want to go. either way I will walk two planes at once. It’s more about ignoring one to live in fear or accept both and exit reality.

I think about you so much. It feels like that’s all I do. Think about you. Making art about you. Hoping you like my art. Fearing the response. Fearing letting go of the fantasy of us. Fearing another failed crush. That I’ll never be enough. I don’t know what that means. How would I ever be enough for someone. How much more could I possibly do? I think I’m enough. I hope you think so too. I hope you think I’m pretty. Do you think I’m pretty?

You don’t think I’m real, do you?

I’m not sure of the answer myself.

It really frightens me,

that question.
